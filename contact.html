Frequently Asked Questions

What types of agricultural businesses do you work with?

We work with a wide range of agricultural businesses, including producers, cooperatives, processors, exporters, importers, and agricultural technology companies. Our services are adaptable to businesses of various sizes, from family farms to large corporations.

How do you typically structure your consulting engagements?

Our engagements typically begin with an initial assessment and strategy development phase, followed by implementation support as needed. We offer both project-based consulting and ongoing advisory relationships depending on your needs.

Do you offer virtual consultations?

Yes, we provide both in-person and virtual consultations to accommodate clients from different geographical locations. Our virtual services include video conferences, digital document sharing, and secure online collaboration tools.

What agricultural commodities do you specialize in?

While we have experience across many agricultural sectors, our primary expertise is in grain (wheat, corn, rice), oilseeds (soybeans, canola), specialty crops, and value-added agricultural products.
