About PJ Cui LLC

Agricultural trade experts with a passion for sustainable growth

Our Story

PJ Cui LLC was founded in 2010 by agricultural economist Dr. Patricia J. Cui, who recognized the need for specialized consulting services in the increasingly complex world of global agricultural trade. After spending two decades working with international agricultural organizations and leading trade initiatives across multiple continents, Dr. Cui established the company to help agricultural businesses navigate global markets effectively.

What began as a boutique consultancy has grown into a respected firm serving clients from small family farms to large agricultural cooperatives and processors. Throughout our growth, we have maintained our commitment to personalized service and practical solutions that deliver measurable results.

Today, PJ Cui LLC is recognized for our deep expertise in agricultural trade markets, particularly in grain, oilseed, and specialty crop sectors. We combine academic rigor with practical experience to provide clients with insights and strategies that help them thrive in a competitive global marketplace.

Our Approach

Data-Driven Insights

We believe in making decisions based on comprehensive data analysis and market intelligence. Our recommendations are grounded in thorough research and empirical evidence.

Practical Solutions

We focus on developing actionable strategies that can be implemented effectively. Our solutions are tailored to each client's specific circumstances, resources, and goals.

Long-Term Relationships

We view our clients as partners and invest in building lasting relationships based on trust and mutual success. Many of our clients have been with us since our founding.

Sustainable Practices

We advocate for environmentally responsible and economically sustainable agricultural trade practices that create value for all stakeholders in the supply chain.

Our Expertise

Agricultural Economics

Our team includes trained agricultural economists who understand the unique market dynamics of agricultural commodities and can forecast trends with precision.

International Trade

We have extensive experience in international trade regulations, agreements, and negotiations specifically related to agricultural products.

Supply Chain Management

Our consultants specialize in optimizing agricultural supply chains from production to distribution, enhancing efficiency and resilience.

Market Development

We help businesses identify and develop new markets for their agricultural products, creating strategies for successful market entry and growth.
